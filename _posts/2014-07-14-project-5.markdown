description: The project brief was to add "smart" Yplan banners to Bookatable emails. This meant that when diners received email confirmation of their restaurant booking they would also receive a highly relevant Yplan link showing interesting events near the restaurant and on the same date as the reservation. While the partnership was ultimately not successful on a commercial basis, it enabled me to gain a strong understanding of Bookatable's C# code base and email-sending infrastructure.
